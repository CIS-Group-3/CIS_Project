WELCOME TO SEICOT

SEICOT stands for "Societal and Economic Impacts of COVID Over Time"

SEICOT is a comprehensive data analysis tool designed to explore the multifaceted impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic across various societal and economic domains. It serves as a versatile analytical platform that caters to diverse user needs, ranging from healthcare professionals and policymakers to investors and law enforcement agencies.

To start, select a tab above to view a different impact of COVID. Each tab will ask for a different specification to view the chart(s).
